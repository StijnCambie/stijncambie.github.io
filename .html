Personal Page of Stijn Cambie

I am Stijn Cambie, a former PhD student of Ross Kang at Raboud University Nijmegen, who graduated (cum laude) the 19th of April 2022.
The title of my thesis is "Extremal aspects of distances and colourings in graphs" and one can find both a full version and a Short version of it.
The introductory talk has been done in Dutch and has been recorded; Lekenpraatje.
My main interests are in extremal problems on various graph parameters and in extremal set theory.
In January 2022, I started a postdoc at Warwick University. From mid May onwards, I continue as a postdoc at the research group from Hong Liu at IBS.
My new homepage can be found by clicking here.
People interested to work together on related things in the future can always contact me.
